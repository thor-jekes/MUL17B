My name is Thor Jekes and and I'm in training as a multimedia designer at CPHBusiness in Lyngby, Denmark. I specialize in graphic design, visual design and branding.

I believe that design is a process, and that through research and exploration, the right solution will present itself.

I've had the privilege to work with Allikelund Gymnasium, Facebook, Operate, Kræftens Bekæmpelse, Johan Borups Højskole, Foot Locker and FSSK.

Currently, I'm editor at FSSK.
